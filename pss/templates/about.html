About Us

The Panyango Secondary School Old Boys' and Old Girls' Association is an alumni organization formed by former students of Panyango Secondary School. The association aims to foster a sense of community among alumni, promote the welfare of the school, and support current students through various initiatives. Here’s a detailed look at the association:

Objectives and Mission

  • Networking and Community Building: The association provides a platform for former students to reconnect, network, and build lasting relationships.
  • Support for Current Students: Through mentorship programs, scholarships, and donations, the association supports the academic and personal development of current students.
  • School Development: Alumni contribute to the development of the school by funding infrastructure projects, providing educational resources, and participating in school events.
  • Preserving Traditions: The association helps to preserve the school’s history and traditions, ensuring that the legacy of Panyango Secondary School continues.

Activities and Initiatives

  • Reunions and Events: Regular reunions and social events are organized to bring alumni together. These events offer opportunities for networking, reminiscing, and discussing ways to support the school.
  • Scholarships and Financial Aid: The association raises funds to provide scholarships and financial aid to deserving students, helping to ease the financial burden on families and encourage academic excellence.
  • Mentorship Programs: Alumni volunteers mentor current students, offering guidance on academic and career choices, and helping them navigate challenges both in school and in their future careers.
  • Fundraising Campaigns: Various fundraising campaigns are conducted to support school projects, such as building new classrooms, renovating facilities, and purchasing educational materials.
  • Community Service: Members of the association engage in community service projects, promoting a spirit of giving back and making a positive impact on the broader community.

Membership

  • Eligibility: Membership is open to all former students of Panyango Secondary School. There are different membership levels, including regular members, life members, and honorary members.
  • Benefits: Members enjoy benefits such as networking opportunities, access to exclusive events, and the chance to contribute to the growth and development of the school.

How to Join

  • Registration: Interested alumni can register by filling out a membership form available on the association’s website or at school events.
  • Membership Fees: There may be a nominal fee to join, which helps fund the association’s activities and initiatives.

Communication and Outreach

  • Website and Social Media: The association maintains an online presence through its website and social media platforms, keeping members informed about upcoming events, news, and opportunities to get involved.
  • Newsletters: Regular newsletters are sent out to members, providing updates on the association’s activities, achievements, and plans.

Impact and Achievements

  • Student Success: Many students have benefited from scholarships and mentorship programs, going on to achieve great success in their academic and professional lives.
  • School Improvements: Fundraising efforts have led to significant improvements in the school’s infrastructure and resources, enhancing the learning environment for current students.
  • Alumni Network: The association has built a strong network of alumni who support each other personally and professionally, creating a vibrant and engaged community.
