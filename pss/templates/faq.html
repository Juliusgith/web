Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

This FAQ will help provide a comprehensive understanding of the Panyango SS OB's and OG's Association, its objectives, activities, and how alumni and other interested individuals can get involved.

General Information

  1. What is Panyango SS OB's and OG's Association?

    2. Panyango SS OB's and OG's Association is an alumni organization for former students of Panyango Secondary School. It aims to foster a sense of community among alumni, support current students, and contribute to the development of the school.

  2. What are the main objectives of the association?

    3. The main objectives include networking and community building among alumni, providing support for current students through scholarships and mentorship, funding school development projects, and preserving the school's traditions and history.

Membership

  1. Who can join the association?

    2. Membership is open to all former students of Panyango Secondary School, regardless of their year of graduation.

  2. How do I become a member?

    3. You can become a member by filling out a membership form available on the association’s website or at school events. There may be a nominal membership fee.

  3. What are the benefits of being a member?

    4. Benefits include networking opportunities, access to exclusive events, the chance to participate in mentorship programs, and the ability to contribute to the growth and development of Panyango Secondary School.

Activities and Initiatives

  1. What kind of events does the association organize?

    2. The association organizes reunions, social events, fundraising campaigns, mentorship programs, and community service projects.

  2. How does the association support current students?

    3. The association supports current students through scholarships, financial aid, and mentorship programs, helping them with academic and personal development.

  3. What types of fundraising activities are conducted?

    4. Fundraising activities include donation drives, charity events, and online campaigns to raise funds for school infrastructure projects, educational materials, and student scholarships.

Contributions and Volunteering

  1. How can I contribute to the association's efforts?

    2. You can contribute by donating funds, volunteering for mentorship programs, participating in fundraising events, and offering your expertise to help with the association’s projects.

  2. Can I volunteer if I am not a member?

    3. Yes, non-members can also volunteer and contribute to various activities and initiatives. However, becoming a member provides additional benefits and opportunities for involvement.

Communication and Updates

  1. How does the association communicate with its members?

    2. The association communicates through its website, social media platforms, newsletters, and emails to keep members informed about upcoming events, news, and opportunities.

  2. How can I stay updated on the association’s activities?

    3. To stay updated, you can follow the association on social media, subscribe to the newsletter, and regularly check the association’s website for announcements and updates.

Impact and Achievements

  1. What impact has the association had on the school and students?

    2. The association has provided scholarships, improved school infrastructure, offered mentorship, and created a strong alumni network, all contributing to the success and well-being of students and the school.

  2. How are the funds raised by the association used?

    3. Funds are used for scholarships, school infrastructure projects, educational resources, and various student support programs.

Contact and Further Information

  1. How can I contact the association for more information?

    2. You can contact the association through their official website’s contact form, email, or social media pages. Contact details are usually provided on the association’s website.
